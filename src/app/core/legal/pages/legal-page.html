Information We Collect

HeyJivu collects the information needed to create your account, authenticate you, run your content workflows, and connect optional services you choose to enable.

  • Account details such as name, email address, login method, and organization membership.
  • User content such as uploaded videos, generated media, templates, prompts, captions, and publishing metadata.
  • Configuration data such as storage settings, AI provider settings, and connected service tokens when you authorize an integration.
  • Operational data such as job status, logs, device/browser metadata, and security events used to keep the service reliable.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the app, process jobs, authenticate users, store files, prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability. We do not sell Google user data or use it for advertising.

Google User Data

Google Sign-In is used for account authentication. Optional Google Drive access is limited to files and folders created by HeyJivu or explicitly selected for use with HeyJivu. Google data is used only to provide user-facing app features that the user requested.

User Control

You can disconnect optional Google integrations from your settings, remove stored credentials where available, or contact us to request account and data deletion.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact admin@heyjivu.com.

Use of HeyJivu

You may use HeyJivu to create, process, store, and publish content that you own or have permission to use. You are responsible for the content and connected accounts you authorize.

Accounts and Security

You must keep your account credentials secure and notify us if you believe your account or connected services have been accessed without permission.

Connected Services

When you connect Google Drive, YouTube, or another third-party service, HeyJivu uses that access only to perform the actions you request. You can revoke access through HeyJivu settings or through the third-party account controls.

Acceptable Use

You may not use HeyJivu to violate laws, infringe rights, bypass platform rules, distribute malicious content, or interfere with the service.

Service Changes

We may update features, limits, pricing, and these terms as the product evolves. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised terms.

Google OAuth Scope Summary

HeyJivu requests the minimum Google access needed for authentication and optional connected workflows. Optional integrations are requested only when a user chooses to connect that service.
